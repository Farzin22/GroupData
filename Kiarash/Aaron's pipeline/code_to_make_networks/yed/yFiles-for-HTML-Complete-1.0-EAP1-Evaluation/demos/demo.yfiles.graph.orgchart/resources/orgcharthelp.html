Organization Chart

Mouse Wheel
Changes the zoom level of the view.
Click on Employee
Selects the employee in both the chart and the Structure View. Shows the corresponding data in the Properties View.

Structure View

Click on Entry
Selects the employee in both the chart and the Structure View. Centers the chart on the employee.

Toolbar

Hide Parent
Hides the parent of the currently-selected employee.
Show Parent
Shows the parent of the currently-selected employee.
Hide Children
Hides the children of the currently-selected employee.
Show Children
Shows the children of the currently-selected employee.
Show All
Shows the chart with all employees.
