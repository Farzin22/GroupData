The IncrementalHierarchicGroup Demo demonstrates how to expand and collapse sub graphs organized in groups and folders.

This demo shows how to keep complex hierarchically organized diagrams clear and straightforward by showing only parts of the entire structure.

The incremental layout approach ensures that the arrangement of the already visible parts of the graph is kept as consistent as possible after expanding or collapsing a group.

Things to try:

  • Click on the +/- button of a group node to expand or collapse it.
